Batman has no superpowers. Instead he relies on an extraordinary combination of scientific expertise, detective ability, peak physical conditioning, and vast wealth. Having trained himself from the age of ten, he mastered over 127 fighting styles and every major scientific discipline — ensuring no one else ever suffers the loss he endured as a child.

His greatest weapon isn't the Batarang or the Batsuit — it's his mind. Widely regarded as DC's greatest tactician, Batman maintains contingency plans against even his closest allies. And through every battle, one rule never breaks: he does not kill.

After his older brother Itachi slaughtered their entire clan, a young Sasuke dedicated his life to a single purpose: revenge. He abandoned his village, sought power through forbidden means, and walked a path that branded him an international criminal — all in pursuit of one man.

The truth shattered everything he believed. Itachi's massacre was an act of sacrifice, not malice. With that revelation, Sasuke's hatred had no target — only a mission. He helped end the Fourth Shinobi World War and returned to Konoha as its "Supporting Shadow."

Kidnapped at six years old to become one of the UNSC's super-soldiers, John-117 was never given a choice. Augmented beyond human limits, sealed inside half a ton of powered armor, and sent against threats that would annihilate entire worlds — he became the man the UNSC calls when everything else has failed.

With over thirty years of active service, Master Chief has earned every UNSC medal except the Prisoner of War Medallion. There is a reason for that: he does not get captured. He survives. He adapts. And then he wins.

Prince Zuko spent three years chasing the Avatar across the world, bearing his father's scar and his father's mission. Banished and told he could only return with honor, he pursued a goal that was never truly his — desperate to earn the approval of a man who had never deserved his loyalty.

Guided by his uncle Iroh and the truth in his own heart, Zuko eventually made the hardest choice: to join the Avatar and oppose the nation he was born into. His journey remains one of animation's most celebrated redemption arcs.
